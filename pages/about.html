About Me

Originally a data analyst, I've always been intrigued by the power of data and its ability to tell compelling stories. But as time went on, I found myself drawn to the creative and dynamic world of front-end development. The idea of bringing data to life through engaging, user-friendly interfaces captivated me. Eager to embark on this new journey, I enrolled in the SheCodes program, where I've been diligently honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through SheCodes, I'm not just learning how to code; I'm learning how to transform data into visually appealing and interactive experiences, opening new doors to innovative storytelling in the digital age.
